What is Vör


Vör is the collective intelligence of people and the things which make their lives better. We focus on facilitating interaction between the individuals and their environment made intelligent. User interaction happens through the Android application and physical switches, cameras, and sensors placed in the office.

FIND PEOPLE
IN YOUR OFFICE

Indoor location technology is used to provide a live map of the office. People who choose to share their location can be seen walking around on the map. If someone you are looking for is having coffee, go join them and share a moment together.

FIND A COMFORTABLE
PLACE TO WORK

The temperature is measured at many points in the office and translated into a heat map of the office. The same could easily be done with noise or air quality, to allow you to find an optimally comfortable location.

FIND AN EMPTY
BATHROOM NEARBY

By applying motion sensors Vör knows whether any limited resources, like toilets or phone booths, are occupied, and can display this information on the office map.

SHARE
GREAT MOMENTS

Great teams celebrate together. Record 30 seconds of video by pressing a button and automatically share the video to others.

SHARE FOOD

Sharing food and drink is the essence of togetherness. A toggle switch near a sharing space can be turned on to indicate that goods are available. A camera updates periodically to show the food and how much is left. The last person to take food can flip the switch off.
