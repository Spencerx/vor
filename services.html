What's Vör


Vör is the collective intelligence of people and the things which make their lives better. We focus on facilitating interaction between individuals and intelligent things nearby. User interaction is through the Android application and physical switches, camera and sensors placed in the office.

FIND PEOPLE
IN YOUR OFFICE

Indoor location technology is used to provide a live map of the office. People who choose to share their location at the moment can be seen walking around on the map. If someone you are looking for is stopped for a coffee, go join them and share a moment together.

FIND A COMFORTABLE
PLACE TO WORK

The temperature at many points in the office is measured and translated into a heat map of the office. This, and seeing which places are busy and occupied, helps you to find a comfortable place to work.

FIND AN EMPTY
BATHROOM NEARBY

Bathroom motion sensors are used to indicate occupancy. If the bathroom near you is full, you can easily see the nearest free bathroom without having to waste time walking around. The air quality in each bathroom is monitored with a methane sensor.

SHARE
GREAT MOMENTS

Great teams celebrate together. Press a button to post the previous 30 seconds recorded by a videocamera to others in the office. Anyone can then individually share this video with the public as for example a Twitter link to the Youtube.

SHARE FOOD

Eating and sharing are the essence of togetherness. A toggle switch near a sharing space can be turned on to indicate that goodies are available. A camera updates periodically to show the food and how much is left. The last person to take food can flip the switch off.
