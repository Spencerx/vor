Vör - a new kind of smart office experience

Vör is an open source tool that offers a new kind of smart office experience by creating seamless connections between people within office space. It provides relevant information about your surroundings – temperature, workspace availability, who’s near you… Vör knows this and much more.

Features of our minimum viable product (MVP)

Indoor positioning system (IPS)

We have used off-the-shelf estimote beacons to create an android application with customized indoor positioning system for our office space.

Context aware content

Users of our application receive relevant information from their surroundings. We value privacy, so data about users or their activities is never stored.

Intranet for IoT

It is a decentralized and vendor-agnostic to find out where people are in your office, find a quiet place to work, find someone to play a game with or check air quality.

Custom sensors

Customized arduino sensors have been built in order to target our specific needs. The picture about is a methane and movement sensor for use in bathrooms in order to show their availability and air quality.
