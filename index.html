NEW AND IMPROVED OPPORTUNITIES FOR COMMUNICATION - HERE AND NOW

Vör is an open source tool that offers a new kind of smart office experience by creating seamless connections between people within office space. It provides relevant information about your surroundings like temperature, workspace availability, who’s near you. Vör knows this and much more.

Connected

Vör allows people to communicate with each others and the office itself.

Aware

Latest information can be broadcasted to listeners by a flick of a switch or a press of a button.

Safe

What happens in Vör stays in Vör. Your privacy is the highest priority.

How

Does it work?

Beacons and sensors such as buttons, cameras and air quality sensors communicate with employees’ phones over your office WIFI. 3D print the parts that suit you or add your own. Everything is open source including the hardware designs - no analytics or other information is shared to the cloud.

As information about your surroundings becomes available or changes, Vör's application displays it to the users. Broadcasted information, such as food becoming available, or someone making a killer pool shot and recording it as a video, is displayed as a message card in the app.

Some information, for instance the location of your colleagues, or the availability of office resources such as the restrooms, is conveniently displayed on the office map. The map can have a heatmap overlay to show you where it is particularly warm or cold, noisy or quiet... Vör gives you a real-time situation awareness about the overall conditions in your office.
