NEW AND IMPROVED OPPORTUNITIES FOR COMMUNICATION - HERE AND NOW!

Vör is an open source tool that offers a new kind of smart office experience by creating seamless connections between people and surroundings within office space. Vör facilitates interaction by providing relevant information like who’s near you, find a quiet place to work, workspace availability and much more.

Connected

Vör provides users with a new level of connectivity. It not only improve communication possibilites with people around but in a way it let you to 'talk' with your office.

Aware

You'll never again miss the opportunity, for example, to try some delicious cake! Be informed about the latest at your office straight away!

Safe

What happens in Vör stays in Vör. Vör is always protecting your privacy. Most information is only in memory - it never stores data about what you do at the office.

How

Does it work?

Beacons and sensors such as buttons, cameras and air quality sensors communicate with employees’ phones over your office WIFI.

3D print the parts that suit you or add your own. Everything is open source including the hardware designs- no analytics or other information is shared to the cloud.

Vör provides users with data about their surroundings on maps and cards. The user can filter to find this and save preferred cards to follow. The cards react immediately to show new information as it becomes available.

The app also includes a general map of the whole office space. You can even watch your co-workers walk around on the map. Now you have full, real-time situation awareness with all the latest information about the overall conditions in your office are just a tap away.
